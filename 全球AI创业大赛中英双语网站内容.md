### **Navigation Tab 3: Competition Guide (Rules & Process)**

*This page serves as the practical manual for teams preparing to enter the competition.*

* **Competition Tracks**  
  The competition focuses strictly on the Artificial Intelligence industry:  
  * **Track 1: AI Basic Technology**: Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing (NLP), Computer Vision, etc.  
  * **Track 2: AI Industry Applications**: Smart Healthcare, Intelligent Transportation, Smart Manufacturing, Fintech, etc.  
  * **Track 3: AI Hardware & Chips**: AI Chips, Intelligent Sensors, etc.  
* **Eligibility & Requirements**  
  * **Team Structure**: Minimum of 3 core members. At least 1 member must hold a Master's degree or above.  
  * **Overseas Requirement**: For overseas projects, at least 1 member must hold an equivalent to an Associate Professor or above at a renowned overseas institution/university, OR hold a senior position in a well-known international enterprise. Must not have returned to China (or returned after 2025).  
  * **Corporate Status**: No team member may have registered a company in Jiangsu Province under the project's name. Teams must plan to register an enterprise in Nanjing within 6 months after the competition.  
  * **Intellectual Property**: Must possess independent and clearly owned IP.  
  * **Priority given to**: Youth talent under 40, foreign talent, past winners of sub-provincial or higher innovation competitions, and experts in "bottleneck" core technologies.  
* **Timeline & Process**  
  * **April 8 \- June 1, 2026 | Project Collection**: Global application portal opens.  
  * **June 2026 | Preliminary Screening**: Expert panel reviews all applications to select the top 50 projects.  
  * **July 2026 | Project Selection**: Evaluating innovation, tech feasibility, and Nanjing industry synergy to select the Top 30\.  
  * **August 2026 | Asia Selection Finals (Singapore)**: 30 finalists pitch in Singapore. 18 Winners chosen.  
  * **October 2026 | Nanjing Grand Finals**: Selected winning enterprises travel to Nanjing for the grand finale and landing ceremony.  
* **Competition Prizes**  
  * **Total Cash Pool**: $9,800 USD for Singapore Finals  
    * **First Prize (2 Winners)**: $1,000 USD each  
    * **Second Prize (6 Winners)**: $800 USD each  
    * **Third Prize (10 Winners)**: $300 USD each  
      *(Note: The separate $10,000 USD Landing Bonus is detailed in the "Discover Nanjing & Policies" tab).*

### **导航页 3: 大赛指南与赛程 (Competition Guide)**

*此页面作为参赛团队的实用手册，包含全部硬性规则与流程。*

* **赛道设置**  
  大赛聚焦人工智能产业，围绕以下核心领域展开：  
  * **人工智能基础技术**: 机器学习、自然语言处理、计算机视觉等。  
  * **人工智能行业应用**: 智慧医疗、智能交通、智能制造、金融科技等。  
  * **人工智能硬件与芯片**: AI芯片、智能传感器等。  
* **参赛对象及要求**  
  * **团队要求**: 核心成员不低于3人。成员中至少有1人具备硕士及以上学位。  
  * **海外背景要求**: 海外项目核心成员中，至少1人在海外著名高校、科研机构担任相当于副教授及以上职务，或在国际知名企业担任高级职务。该成员需尚未回国（来华）或2025年后回国（来华）。  
  * **落地意愿**: 团队中任何成员尚未以该项目名义在江苏省内注册公司。  
  * **知识产权**: 项目拥有自主知识产权且权属明晰。  
  * **优先支持**: 40周岁以下青年人才、外籍人才、副省级以上双创赛事获奖者，及从事“卡脖子”关键技术研究的人才。  
* **赛程安排**  
  * **2026年4月8日 \- 6月1日 | 项目征集**: 开放全球项目报名入口。  
  * **2026年6月 | 项目初筛**: 专家评审团综合评议，筛选出 50 个项目。  
  * **2026年7月 | 项目遴选**: 评估创新能力、落地可行性与产业链契合度，甄选 30 个项目晋级。  
  * **2026年8月 | 亚洲选拔赛决赛 (新加坡)**: 30家晋级项目在新加坡路演，决出 18 个获奖项目。  
  * **2026年10月 | 南京总决赛**: 组织获奖企业赴南京参加总决赛与落地对接。  
* **赛事优胜奖金设置**  
  * **赛事总奖金**: 9,800 美元 (不含落地奖励)  
    * **一等奖 (2名)**: 每名 1,000 美元  
    * **二等奖 (6名)**: 每名 800 美元  
    * **三等奖 (10名)**: 每名 300 美元  
      *(注：额外的1万美元落地奖励已在“魅力南京与落地政策”版块中详细说明)*
